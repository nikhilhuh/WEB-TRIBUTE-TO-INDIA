Digital Freedom Gallery

Mahatma Gandhi – Father of the Nation, who led India’s independence through non-violence. His ideals now inspire the pursuit of self-reliance in the digital era, free from technological dependence on external powers.

Bhagat Singh – A fearless revolutionary who fought for India’s freedom. His courage inspires today’s youth to protect digital rights, online freedom of speech, and equal access to information.

Rani Lakshmibai – The Queen of Jhansi who became a symbol of resistance in 1857. Her fight for sovereignty echoes in the defense of digital independence and cyber security for the nation.

ISRO – The Indian Space Research Organisation, building indigenous satellites and missions like Mangalyaan and Chandrayaan. This strengthens India’s digital sovereignty and data independence from space.

Unified Payments Interface – A global model for instant, free digital payments. UPI empowers citizens with financial autonomy and strengthens India’s digital economy.

Smart Cities Mission – Building connected urban spaces with AI, IoT, and data-driven planning. Symbolizes freedom to live in safe, sustainable, and digitally intelligent environments.

Young Innovators – Developing AI, blockchain, and VR solutions that secure privacy, expand access, and shape India’s leadership in the global digital space.

Digital Education – Empowering students across India with free online learning platforms and open educational resources, ensuring knowledge reaches every corner.
